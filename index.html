About Me

I was raised in a small town just outside of Ann Arbor, Michigan. I was surrounded by tractors on my grandpa's farm all my life and fell in love with tractors from a very young age. I have many fond memories of fall hayrides on my grandpa's farm as a kid and sitting around roasting marshmallows.

I attended college at Michigan State University where I got an undergraduate degree in Human Biology with a minor in Nutritional Sciences. After college I discovered naturopathic medicine and decided to attend Bastyr University in Kenmore, Washington.

In the summer of 2016 I packed up all of my things from Michigan and headed out West with my boyfriend at the time. After two long years in the program I decided it wasn't for me and began looking at other career options. I had many conversations with my aunt who is a web designer and started getting me into web development! I fell in love immediately and am so excited to be pursuing this dream!
